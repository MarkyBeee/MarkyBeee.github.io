About

We are proudly based in the good ole' US of A, hailing from the fine village of Philadelphia in Pennsylvania. Powerlifting and lifting weights until our armpits sweat is our "thing", and over the years, we've learned that equipment and accessories can get expensive really fast. So we kept that in mind, and are offering budget friendly accessories, without sacrificing performance or quality. All items that we offer has been tested by us and other local, non affiliated powerlifters to ensure that they will hold up to the abuse of daily training.
